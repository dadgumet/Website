🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Bible Barnyard provides free biblical content with no strings attached. Your privacy is important to us.

📖 Who We Are

Bible Barnyard (biblebarnyard.com) provides free, faith-based educational videos and music for children. We operate in the spirit of Matthew 10:8 ("Freely you have received; freely give"). All our content is provided free of charge, always will be, and comes with no strings attached.

Contact: crew@biblebarnyard.com

✨ Our Privacy Promise

We do NOT collect, store, or track any personal information from visitors to our website.

You can:

  • Watch our videos
  • Listen to our music
  • Browse our website
  • Share our content

All without providing any personal information whatsoever.

🚫 What We Don't Do

Bible Barnyard does NOT:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or any personal information
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Track your browsing behavior
  • Store your IP address or location data
  • Require registration or sign-up
  • Sell, share, or trade any information
  • Use advertising networks
  • Collect payment information (everything is free)

🔗 Third-Party Platforms

Our content is shared on third-party platforms to reach more families. These platforms may collect their own information when you use their services:

YouTube:

  • Our videos are hosted on YouTube
  • YouTube may collect information when you watch videos
  • See YouTube's Privacy Policy for their practices

Pinterest:

  • We share our content on Pinterest to help families discover our videos
  • Pinterest may collect information when you view or interact with our pins
  • See Pinterest's Privacy Policy for their practices

Important: We do not control these third-party services and are not responsible for their privacy practices. When you use YouTube, Pinterest, or any other platform where our content appears, you are subject to their privacy policies, not ours.

📧 Contacting Us

If you choose to email us at crew@biblebarnyard.com, we will only use your email address to respond to your message. We do not add email addresses to mailing lists, share them with others, or use them for any other purpose.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our content is designed for children, but we do not collect any information from anyone—children or adults. Parents can feel confident that their children can enjoy our videos and music without any privacy concerns from Bible Barnyard.

However, please supervise children when they use third-party platforms like YouTube or Pinterest, as those services have their own data collection practices.

🌐 Links to Other Websites

Our website may link to YouTube, Pinterest, and other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

📝 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect your information, we have no way to notify you of changes—we encourage you to check back periodically.

❓ Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at crew@biblebarnyard.com.

📌 Summary

Bible Barnyard collects zero personal information. We provide free biblical content for children with absolutely no strings attached. Watch, listen, enjoy, and share—all without giving us any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected when using our website.
